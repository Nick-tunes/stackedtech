Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Overview

StackedTech Automation ("the App") is a personal content automation tool used to post tech and gadget content to TikTok via the TikTok Content Posting API. This app is not a public-facing service and does not collect personal data from third parties.

Information We Collect

The App only collects the minimum information necessary to authenticate with TikTok on behalf of the account owner. This includes OAuth tokens provided by TikTok upon authorization. No personal data from viewers or followers is collected or stored.

How We Use Information

Authentication tokens are used solely to post content to the authorized TikTok account (@StackedTech). They are not shared with any third party, sold, or used for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

This app interacts with TikTok's API. Please refer to TikTok's Privacy Policy for information on how TikTok handles data.

Data Retention

No personal data is stored by the App beyond what is required for the current session's API authentication.

Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at the email associated with the developer account.
